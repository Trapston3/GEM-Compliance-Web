## Non-negotiable decisions

1. Use a calm industrial utility visual language with solid, high-contrast surfaces.
2. Skip broad Liquid Glass/backdrop-filter effects because the target includes old office computers and dense scrolling data.
3. Keep submission pending and acceptance not accepted visually distinct.
4. Treat text notes as note presence/absence, not pass/fail.
5. Show owner information on tender cards only for superusers viewing tenders they do not own.
6. Implement and preview one phase at a time; stop for explicit user sign-off after every Preview URL.
7. Do not promote the final redesign to production until all seven phases are signed off.
